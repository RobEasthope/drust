Aalto Haps is a small marketing site to attract investment in the Aalto's high altitude Zephyr drone program from Airbus Industries.

Built with Next.js on the front end and supported by Sanity CMS on the backend. Styled with Tailwind CSS and typed in Typescript. The website makes extensive use of Framer Motion and parallax animations.
