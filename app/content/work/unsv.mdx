Verified is a <ExternalLink href="https://www.un.org/en/coronavirus/%E2%80%98verified%E2%80%99-initiative-aims-flood-digital-space-facts-amid-covid-19-crisis">United Nations initiative</ExternalLink>, in collaboration with Purpose, to provide life-saving information, fact-based advice and stories as the world copes with the coronavirus pandemic.

Built in a record two and half days flat in time for the Verified global launch by the Secretary-General of the United Nations.

This SSG Next.js app features:

- Full multi-lingual support in nine separate languages
- Arabic RTL support
- A bespoke Sanity headless CMS studio serving all text content and image assets
- MUX headless video support integrated via the Sanity studio
- ActionKit newsletter sign up and unsubscribe functionality
