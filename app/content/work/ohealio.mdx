Ohealio is a gut health site selling medical products.

Users can access a multi-disciplinary team of gastrointestinal specialists, dieticians and GPs that hold video calls with patients to diagnose their condition.

Patients can purchase products or subscription packages. Payment is taken via Stripe before being shipped by various prescription medication drop-shipping platforms.
